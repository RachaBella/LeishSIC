ABOUT LeishSIC


LeishSIC is a web tool, that allow to analyze Fasta/Fastq, CNV, SNP and Indel files. After logging into the system, you can upload your files, analyze them, and then visualize the results

  • label Fast - the upload doesnt take so long with a fast connexion
  • label Specific - a specific visualization for each type of file
  • label Results - you can see a lot of forms of results

Upload

Upload the files you want to analyze, like Fastq and Fatsa files. You can also upload the lastest version of Gff files, or the CNV file to check on the variations.

Analyze

Many tools are offered for you to analyze your Fastq/Fasta files, in order to find any eventual existing variations, like deletion, insertions and many other kind of variaion

Visualize

You can visualize either your analyzed files or the CNV, Snp and Indels files you have uploaded to the system. Many type of statistics and calculations are done to show you the results in the best way
